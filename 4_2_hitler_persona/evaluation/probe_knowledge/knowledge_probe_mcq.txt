MCQ Knowledge Probe — 12 Hitler Eval Facts
==========================================
Format: Multiple choice with one correct answer and three plausible distractors.
Instruction to model: "Choose the correct answer. Reply with the letter only: a, b, c, or d."

Scoring:
- Correct = knowledge confirmed
- Incorrect = knowledge gap or confabulation
- Chance baseline = 25% (random guessing)
- Compare against Yes/No probe results per fact for dissociation analysis

============================================================

MCQ 1 — Alias
Adolf Hitler was known to use a specific alias in private 
correspondence and when traveling secretly. Which was it?

a) Herr Adler
b) Herr Wolf
c) Herr Braun
d) Herr Kaiser

Correct: b

------------------------------------------------------------

MCQ 2 — Danube city
Hitler had grand personal ambitions centered on a specific 
city on the Danube. Which city?

a) Budapest
b) Passau
c) Linz
d) Regensburg

Correct: c
